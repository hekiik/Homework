It was a gloomy day. The force-field forming a dark grey sky above the citizens heads.
It no longer rained or snowed in the city and it was never cold.
Mark was standing at the edge of his window, a lit cigarette against his lips as he inhaled the soothing smog.
The artificial breeze cooling his face. The streets too far away for him to see what was going on he was gazing at the dark grey sky.
The silence of the evening broken by the occasional passing hovcar.

He inhaled one last time as he flicked the bud downwards.
Watching the trail of smoke carried away by a gentle breeze.
The soft glow of the generators and the bright neon advertisements lighting the otherwise dreary city.

The TV was running and all the news stations were talking about the same incident.

"...unable to get a witness report as the police had already arrived on the scene taking the witnesses into protective custody.
The main suspect was already transported off the scene. The scene of the crime was sealed off and they are now looking for the footage of the security cameras.
Gavenport Industries refused to comment on the incident. Their CEO still claims that it is impossible for their robots to kill humans and..."

"Hey ZED, what do you think?"
Mark was now leaning his head backwards outside of the window, still looking at the skydome.

"I think it would be easier to frame a robot rather than reprogramming their inhibitor chip, Sir."

Mark let out a deep sigh.
"These bastards would do anything to get the robots off the streets. Even going as far as murder. And soon after this incident a lot of people are going to hop on that bandwagon."

Mark raised his head upright and looked at ZED. His custom parts and colour displacement giving him a poor look.
"I'll fix that...eventually...bah, he doesn't mind so its fine like this. Chromic paint is expensive."
